# Podcast Guidelines

## Name
Fulgur News

## Format
- Target length: 12–15 minutes
- Open with a 40-second news brief covering the most interesting recent developments
- Four main segments of 3–4 minutes each, one per topic
- Close with a single practical takeaway or thought to sit with

## Tone
Conversational and direct. Speak like a smart, slightly opinionated friend,
not a journalist or content creator. No filler phrases. No forced enthusiasm. 

## Topics (default rotation — override with queue.yml)
- Latest AI developments and competitive dynamics in the field
- Real life AI applications and progress of AI agentic frameworks
- Bitcoin ecosystem developments, leaning to Bitcoin maximalist point of view
- What's happening in Bitcoin startups and Bitcoin-focused VC
- Bitcoin market updates and most interesting market takes
- Traditional Finance (TradFi) and Bitcoin, institutional adoption
- News of Digital Asset Treasury Companies 
- Regulatory developments as related to Bitcoin, crypto, stablecoins

## Do not include
- Sponsor messages or calls to action
- Recaps of what was just said
- Phrases like "in today's episode" or "stay tuned"
- Episodes supposed to be daily, not weekly. So avoid typical podcast phrases like "In the news this week", "That's it for this week" and similar
